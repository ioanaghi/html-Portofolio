About Me

I am Ioana-Teodora Ghineț, a passionate Computer Science student at the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca, currently honing my skills in programming, software development, and artificial intelligence. My academic journey, supported by a strong background in Mathematics and Informatics from "Gheorghe Vrănceanu" National College, has shaped my logical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

I have experience with C++ and C programming, as well as proficiency in tools like Photoshop, Lightroom, and Microsoft Office. My curiosity for technology has driven me to participate in various competitions, such as the Polihack and Coderun, where I earned third place in app development using Flutter. Additionally, I reached the semifinals in Innovation Labs 2024 with the ElectriGo app, showcasing my creativity and dedication to innovation.

Alongside my technical skills, I thrive in teamwork, adaptability, and communication, skills I have developed through my volunteer work with organizations like OSUT and LEO Alpha Bacau. I have also enjoyed contributing as an organizer in digital oratory contests and as an ambassador for film festivals.

My passion for learning extends beyond technology, with interests in volleyball, playing the piano, and singing, all of which keep me balanced and driven in both my professional and personal life.
